Frequently Asked Questions

Is it really free?

Yes! The extension is free to download. We offer a Demo Mode with 10 free daily chats (powered by our keys). For unlimited usage, you can bring your own API keys (BYOK) from OpenAI, Anthropic, or others.

Is my API key safe?

Absolutely. Your API keys are encrypted and stored locally in your browser's secure storage. We do not have servers, and your keys never leave your device except to communicate directly with the AI provider.

Which models are supported?

We support all major providers: OpenAI (GPT-4o, o1), Anthropic (Claude 3.5 Sonnet), Google (Gemini 1.5 Pro), DeepSeek, Azure OpenAI, and any OpenAI-compatible custom endpoint (like Ollama or LM Studio).
